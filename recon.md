## Current State

Iron Tribe Fitness Gym is a community-centered strength and coaching gym in Malvar, Batangas — a first-class municipality of 74,565 people. The business has an established physical presence and a coherent brand identity: **supportive, high-quality fitness for students and adults.** It operates with a more sophisticated offer than its digital footprint communicates.

The gym's digital presence lives almost entirely on Facebook and Instagram. Facebook is the stronger trust signal — it surfaces the full address, contact info, and social proof. Instagram shows brand personality and content cadence (47 posts as of mid-2026). Neither channel functions as a conversion system.

**Offer stack (publicly discoverable):**
- Monthly membership (price post-March 2026 update unconfirmed)
- Free body assessment / body composition assessment
- Annual membership: ₱8,000 regular / ₱6,000 student (freeze policy: 2x/year, max 1 month each)
- Twice-weekly coach check-ins referenced in indexed posts
- Daily and weekly access options (exact prices unconfirmed post-March 2026)

**Content pillars observed:** fitness education, member/community recognition, gym etiquette, seasonal promos, cultural/humor content. Cadence is active but not yet programmatic.

## Infrastructure Recommendations

**Layer 1 — Public Presence (Priority: Critical)**
Premium website with 10 pages. Hero with local trust signals. Clear offer architecture. Filipino payment UX.

**Layer 2 — Lead Capture (Priority: Critical)**
Assessment booking form → CRM intake. Join Now flow with GCash/Maya/card checkout. Lead source tracking.

**Layer 3 — Operations (Priority: High)**
Member CRM with coach assignment. Membership pipeline with stage tracking. Class booking system.

**Layer 4 — Growth (Priority: Medium)**
Google Business Profile optimization. Review request engine. Referral system. Challenge module.

**Layer 5 — Intelligence (Priority: Medium)**
Owner dashboard: KPIs, churn risk, next actions. Revenue tracking. Lead source performance.
